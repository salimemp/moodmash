# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** January 17, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of MoodMash services. By using MoodMash, you agree to comply with this policy.

## 2. Acceptable Use

### 2.1 Intended Use

MoodMash is designed for:
- Personal mood tracking and journaling
- Mental wellness monitoring
- Health metric tracking
- Social support and community engagement

### 2.2 Age Requirements

- Users must be 13 years or older (16 in EEA)
- Users under 18 may require parental consent
- Health data features require adult (18+) status

### 2.3 Account Responsibilities

You agree to:
- Provide accurate information
- Maintain security of your account
- Notify us of unauthorized access
- Use only one personal account

## 3. Prohibited Activities

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

You may NOT use MoodMash for:
- Violating any laws or regulations
- Infringing intellectual property rights
- Fraud or deceptive practices
- Money laundering or financial crimes
- Harassment, stalking, or threats

### 3.2 Harmful Content

You may NOT post or share:
- Content promoting self-harm or suicide
- Hate speech or discrimination
- Violent or graphic content
- Sexually explicit material
- Misinformation about health or medicine
- Content harmful to minors

### 3.3 System Abuse

You may NOT:
- Attempt to gain unauthorized access
- Circumvent security measures
- Overload systems (DDoS, spam)
- Scrape or harvest data
- Reverse engineer the service
- Use automated tools without permission
- Interfere with other users

### 3.4 Data Misuse

You may NOT:
- Share others' personal information without consent
- Access others' accounts or data
- Sell or transfer account access
- Use data for unauthorized purposes
- Violate privacy of other users

### 3.5 Commercial Restrictions

Without prior written permission, you may NOT:
- Sell or commercialize MoodMash data
- Use MoodMash for competing services
- Advertise or solicit users
- Sublicense access to others

## 4. Content Guidelines

### 4.1 Your Content

You retain ownership of content you create (moods, journals, etc.).

You grant MoodMash a license to:
- Store and process your content
- Provide the service features
- Generate insights and analytics
- Improve our services (anonymized)

### 4.2 Sharing Content

When sharing with others (friends, groups):
- Respect others' privacy
- Do not share sensitive information without consent
- Be supportive and respectful
- Report concerning content

### 4.3 Community Standards

In social features:
- Be kind and supportive
- Respect different experiences
- Maintain confidentiality
- Report policy violations

## 5. Health and Safety

### 5.1 Not Medical Advice

MoodMash is NOT:
- A substitute for professional medical advice
- A diagnostic tool
- Emergency services

**If you are in crisis, contact emergency services or a crisis hotline.**

### 5.2 Wellness Intent

MoodMash is designed to:
- Support self-awareness
- Encourage healthy habits
- Complement (not replace) professional care
- Provide educational information

### 5.3 Reporting Concerns

If you see content indicating:
- Self-harm or suicide risk
- Abuse or exploitation
- Mental health crisis

Please report immediately and encourage professional help.

## 6. Security

### 6.1 Your Responsibilities

- Use strong, unique passwords
- Enable two-factor authentication
- Keep your devices secure
- Log out on shared devices
- Report security concerns

### 6.2 Prohibited Security Actions

- Probing for vulnerabilities without permission
- Attempting to bypass authentication
- Accessing others' accounts
- Distributing malware
- Exploiting bugs for unauthorized access

### 6.3 Responsible Disclosure

If you discover a security vulnerability:
- Report to security@moodmash.win
- Do not exploit or disclose publicly
- We will acknowledge and investigate
- Responsible disclosure may be rewarded

## 7. API Usage

### 7.1 Rate Limits

- 100 requests per minute (standard)
- 1000 requests per minute (premium)
- Exceeding limits may result in throttling

### 7.2 API Terms

- Use only documented endpoints
- Include proper authentication
- Cache responsibly
- Handle errors gracefully
- Do not overburden systems

## 8. Enforcement

### 8.1 Violations

Policy violations may result in:
- Warning notification
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent account termination
- Legal action
- Reporting to authorities

### 8.2 Appeals

To appeal an enforcement action:
- Email: appeals@moodmash.win
- Include account details and circumstances
- We will review within 10 business days

### 8.3 Reporting

Report violations to:
- In-app reporting features
- Email: abuse@moodmash.win
- Reports are confidential

## 9. Changes

We may update this policy:
- With 30 days notice for material changes
- Immediately for legal/safety requirements
- Continued use constitutes acceptance
