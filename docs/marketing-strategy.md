## 1. Budget-Friendly Marketing Strategy

- "Mental Health Tips for Busy Professionals"
- "The Science Behind Mood and Productivity"
- "How AI Can Help You Understand Your Emotions"

- Answer questions on Quora and Reddit (r/mentalhealth, r/selfimprovement)

- Optimize app store listing (ASO)

**Keywords to Target:**
- mood tracker app
- mental health app
- emotion tracking
- wellness app
- mood journal
- anxiety tracker
- depression management app

**Corporate Wellness Programs:**

### E. Product Hunt Launch (Free)

**Pre-Launch (2 weeks before):**
- Build anticipation on social media
- Create a ship page
- Gather email subscribers
- Prepare high-quality visuals
- Write compelling description

**Launch Day:**
- Post at 12:01 AM PST
- Engage with every comment
- Share across all channels
- Offer launch-day perks (lifetime discounts)
- Ask supporters to upvote

**Post-Launch:**
- Thank the community
- Share results and metrics
- Continue engaging with new users
- Implement feedback quickly

---

### F. App Store Optimization (Free)

**Optimization Checklist:**
- Compelling app title with keywords
- Keyword-rich description
- High-quality screenshots (all slots)
- App preview video
- Regular updates to boost ranking
- Encourage positive reviews

**Keywords for ASO:**
- Primary: mood tracker, mental health
- Secondary: wellness, emotion, journal
- Long-tail: daily mood tracker free, AI mood analysis

**Review Strategy:**
- Ask satisfied users to leave reviews
- Respond to all reviews (positive and negative)
- Address issues mentioned in reviews
- Update app based on feedback

## 3. Target Audience

### Primary Audiences:

**Young Adults (18-35)**
- Struggling with stress, anxiety, or depression
- Tech-savvy and app-friendly
- Value self-improvement
- Active on social media

**Self-Improvement Enthusiasts**
- Already tracking habits
- Interested in productivity
- Willing to pay for quality tools
- Engage with content

**Therapy Clients**
- Currently in therapy
- Looking for tracking tools
- May be recommended by therapists
- Higher conversion potential

### Secondary Audiences:

**Mental Health Professionals**
- Therapists and counselors
- May recommend to clients
- Potential B2B opportunity

**Corporate Wellness Programs**
- HR departments
- Employee wellness initiatives
- Enterprise potential

**Educational Institutions**
- Universities and colleges
- Student wellness centers
- High potential for adoption
