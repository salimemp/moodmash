## Risks and Mitigation

### Risk 1: Device Compatibility
**Probability:** High | **Impact:** Medium

**Description:** Not all devices support AR features.

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Progressive enhancement (fall back to 2D/video)
- WebXR for broadest compatibility
- Clear device requirements communication
- Pre-flight compatibility check in app
- Alternative non-AR experiences

### Risk 2: High Development Costs
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High

**Description:** AR development is expensive and time-consuming.

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Start with WebXR (lower cost than native)
- Use asset marketplaces for 3D content
- Phased rollout based on ROI
- Consider AR development partners
- Reuse assets across experiences

### Risk 3: User Adoption
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** High

**Description:** Users may not adopt AR features.

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Compelling onboarding tutorials
- Clear value proposition messaging
- Free trial of AR features
- Social proof (user testimonials, reviews)
- Gamification incentives

### Risk 4: Performance Issues
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium

**Description:** AR experiences may lag or drain battery.

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Aggressive optimization (LOD, occlusion culling)
- Asset streaming (load on demand)
- Quality settings (low/medium/high)
- Battery saver mode
- Performance monitoring and alerts

### Risk 5: Motion Sickness
**Probability:** Low | **Impact:** Medium

**Description:** Some users may experience discomfort.

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Comfort mode options
- Gradual introduction to AR
- Static reference points
- Session duration recommendations
- Clear warnings and opt-out

### Risk 6: Content Creation Bottleneck
**Probability:** Medium | **Impact:** Medium

**Description:** Creating quality 3D content takes time.

**Mitigation Strategies:**
- Partner with 3D content creators
- Use procedural generation where possible
- User-generated content (future)
- Prioritize highest-impact environments
- Reusable component library

### Accessibility Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| Voice Control | "Open forest environment" |
| High Contrast Mode | Increased visual clarity |
| Reduced Motion | Minimal animations |
| Audio Descriptions | Describe visual elements |

- Stable horizon reference
- Gradual transitions (no sudden movements)
- User-controlled movement speed
- Seated mode option
- Session duration warnings (every 10 min)
- Comfort break reminders
